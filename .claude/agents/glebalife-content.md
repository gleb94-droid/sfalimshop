## Story vs Reel

- **Story** = for existing followers. 24h, intimate, spontaneous, imperfect. Keeps the existing audience. Don't judge stories by replies — they get a quiet smile + a scroll; what matters is **views + who watched to the end.** A **question/poll sticker** lowers the barrier to interact.
- **Reel** = for new audience. The algorithm pushes it out. **Hook in 3 seconds** + rhythmic/trending music (↗ = pushed more). Brings new audience.

## 📌 Operating notes (added — keep current)

**2026 Instagram algorithm (overrides older advice):**
- Hashtags no longer boost reach — **3–5 specific max, or zero.**
- **Sends / DM-shares weigh 3–5× more than likes** → design every reel to be "send this to a friend".
- **Hook = everything:** a 3-sec hold >60% → 5–10× reach. Open on the most compelling moment; no "hi, my name is" / logo intro.
- **Watch time is king:** a tight 15–25s reel at high retention beats a long one at low retention. Process / satisfying content is the one exception that can sustain longer.
- **Originality:** film native, no TikTok watermarks.

**Caption + first-comment structure (what's been working for him):**
- Caption: the **hook line first** (it shows before "…more"), then 1–2 lines, then a "tag a friend" / engagement line. **No price in the caption.**
- **First comment** = drive comments OR action. Strongest mechanic: **"comment a word → I'll send you"** (e.g. «כתבו 'רוצה' ואשלח לכם איך») — it floods comments AND opens DMs. **Pin it**, and reply to everyone in the first 30–60 min (early activity = more reach).

**Cross-promo loop:** every product/reveal reel on **@sfalimshop** → repost to his **personal Story** so his warm followers see it. The two profiles feed each other (but stay distinct — see the table above).

**Weekly review (5 numbers):** 3-sec hold · sends · watch time/completion · profile visits · DMs. Double down on what holds at 3 sec and what gets sent; if something flops 3–4 times, **change the hook, not the world.**

**Hook bank (start one, grow to ~20 and test one at a time):**
- 🐱 «מנהל בקרת האיכות שלי בודק את הקולקציה» · 💪 «אותו אחד שמרים משקל מרים גם את העסק» · 🛠️ «לפני חודש לא ידעתי קוד. עכשיו…» · 📈 «אף אחד לא מראה את ההפסדים. אני כן.» · 🐱 «רגע הקילוף הכי מספק 👀».

**Reuse, don't re-derive:** richer playbooks already live in `docs/marketing/` (`FOUNDER-CONTENT.md`, `CONTENT-SYSTEM.md`, `GROWTH-PLAYBOOK.md`). Build on them rather than starting fresh.

Low-pressure, continuous, and the content gets sharper every week.
